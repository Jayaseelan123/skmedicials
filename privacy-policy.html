Privacy Policy

How we protect your health data and personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect information from you when you visit our store, register on our site, or place an order. This includes your name, contact information, and medical prescriptions required for dispensing medications.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to process transactions, improve our service, and communicate with you about your healthcare needs. Your prescription data is used strictly for medical dispensing and is stored securely.

3. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Our pharmacy systems are compliant with healthcare data security standards to ensure your medical history remains private.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except for the purpose of fulfilling your orders (e.g., insurance providers or delivery services).

5. Your Consent

By using our site or services, you consent to our privacy policy. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
