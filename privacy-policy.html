Privacy Policy

How we protect your health data and personal information.

1. Information Collection & Use

At Saikalai Medicals, we collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and delivery address to process your online orders. For prescription-only medications, we securely collect and store digital copies of your medical prescriptions as required by pharmacy regulations.

This data is used exclusively for order fulfillment, verifying medication safety, and communicating important health-related updates or refill reminders.

2. Prescription Data Privacy

Your medical prescriptions and health history are treated with the highest level of confidentiality. Access is restricted to our licensed pharmacists and authorized healthcare personnel. We do not share your medical records with third parties unless required by law or for healthcare insurance processing.

3. Online Payment & Security

For online orders, we use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your transaction details. While we facilitate payments through secure third-party gateways, Saikalai Medicals does not store your full credit card or bank account numbers on our servers.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We share your contact and delivery information with our trusted logistics partners solely for the purpose of shipping your medications. We ensure these partners adhere to strict data protection standards. We do not sell or trade your personal information for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain prescription records for the period mandated by national pharmacy laws. You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal account information at any time through our website or by contacting our support team.
