Terms & Conditions

Rules and guidelines for using our pharmacy services.

1. Prescription Requirements

In compliance with legal regulations, Saikalai Medicals will only dispense medications categorized as "Schedule H," "H1," or "X" drugs upon receipt of a valid medical prescription from a registered medical practitioner.

All uploaded prescriptions are verified by our licensed pharmacists. We reserve the right to reject any order if the prescription is found to be invalid, expired, or tampered with.

2. Online Order Process

By placing an order on our website, you agree to provide accurate health and contact information. Orders are confirmed only after successful prescription verification (if applicable) and payment processing. We may contact you via phone or email for order clarification or health counseling.

3. Return and Refund Policy

For safety and hygiene reasons, medications cannot be returned once delivered. Returns or refunds are only applicable if the products are damaged during transit, expired at the time of delivery, or if the wrong medication was sent. Please report such issues within 24 hours of delivery.

4. Delivery Terms

We strive for timely delivery; however, timelines are estimates. Special handling (like cold-chain storage) is maintained for sensitive medications during transit. It is the customer's responsibility to ensure someone is available to receive the delivery at the provided address.

5. Medical Disclaimer

The information on this website is for general health awareness and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor before starting any medication. Saikalai Medicals is not liable for any self-medication consequences.
